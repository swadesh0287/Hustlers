Hustlers On Fire


Our primary objective is to reduce unskilled freelancers gap in all around the world. Hustler is a digital platform for freelancers just like social media website where people can showcase their work and utilize their skills. Users can easily build relationship with clients and work for them. Clients can go through the profiles of the hustlers to find the suitable match according to their need. Users and clients can chat with each other to discuss about the work. Payment will be taken by the clients in advance by our platform so that there will be no fraud cases. When hustler will complete the work they will receive the payment in their accounts after deducting 10% of the total amount.
